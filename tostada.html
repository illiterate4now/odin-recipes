Tostada

Description

This recipe cosists of a corn tortilla that is fried and topped with beans, rice and your choice of beef or chicken.

Ingredients:

  • Chicken or Beef
  • Cliantro
  • Refried Beans

Steps:

  1. Fry your corn tortilla
  2. Fry your beans
  3. Cook your chicken or beef
  4. Pile yout beans and meat onto the fried tortilla and top with cilantro
