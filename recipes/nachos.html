Nachos

Description:

Corn chips with your choice of chicken, beef, or pork topped with nacho cheese and any veggie you would like.

Ingredients:

  • chicken, beef, or pork
  • corn chips
  • nacho cheese
  • lettuce
  • tomato

Steps:

  1. Place chips on plate
  2. Prepare meat and place over chips
  3. Drizzle nacho cheese over meat and chips
  4. Add veggies on top
