Taquito

Description

This is a fried and tighly rolled corn tortilla stuffed with chicken or pork. It is usually dipped in sauce.

Ingredients:

  • corn tortilla
  • chicken or pork
  • dipping sauce

Steps:

  1. Prepare chicken or pork
  2. Lightly fry tortilla
  3. Stuff meat into tortilla, roll, and fry again
  4. Dip in sauce and enjoy
